Bio

I am currently a fourth-year PhD Student with the Depintelligence Software Lab, School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering, Beihang University (BUAA), Beijing, China. My supervisor is Prof. Zheng Zheng. From 09/2018 to 12/2018, I was a Visiting PhD Student at School of Software, University of Technology Sydney (UTS), under the supervision of Dr. Yulei Sui. My research interests broadly include software reliability and empirical software engineering.
