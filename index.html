Bio

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. Student with the Depintelligence Software Lab, School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering, Beihang University (BUAA), Beijing, China. My supervisor is Prof. Zheng Zheng. I was a Visiting Ph.D. Student at the School of Software, University of Technology Sydney (UTS), under the supervision of Dr. Yulei Sui.

My current research interest focuses on interdisciplinary studies in (empirical) software engineering, such as using network science for exploring latent and interesting patterns in the evolution of software systems, using machine learning for mining software repositories (e.g., codes, commits, and bugs).

I am looking for a faculty job in university. I will obtained my Ph.D. degree later this year.
