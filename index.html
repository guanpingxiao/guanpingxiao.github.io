Bio

Guanping Xiao is currently an assistant professor of the College of Computer Science and Technology in Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA), China. He obtained his Ph.D. degree from Beihang University (BUAA), China, in January 2020. His Ph.D. supervisor is Prof. Zheng Zheng. During his Ph.D. studies, he was a visiting Ph.D. student at School of Computer Science, University of Technology Sydney (UTS), under the supervision of Dr. Yulei Sui.

His research interest focuses on interdisciplinary studies in (empirical) software engineering, such as using network science for exploring latent and interesting patterns in the evolution of software systems, using deep learning for mining software repositories (e.g., codes, commits, and bugs), with the aim of improving software quality and reliability.
