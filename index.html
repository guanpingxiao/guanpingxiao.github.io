Bio

Guanping Xiao is an assistant professor of the College of Computer Science and Technology in Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA), China. He obtained his Ph.D. degree from Beihang University (BUAA), China, in January 2020. His Ph.D. advisor is Prof. Zheng Zheng. From September to December 2018, he was a visiting student at School of Computer Science, University of Technology Sydney (UTS), where he was under supervision of Prof. Yulei Sui. His research interest focuses on intelligent software reliability.
